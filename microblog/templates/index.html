I'm Will!

They say that "Where there's a Will, there's a way."

I've always liked that phrase, for obvious reasons. But it does seem a little out-dated. I mean, where? This is the internet. Where isn't really that important. (Though, FYI, I'm in San Francisco.)

When though, now that does mean something. You never know when you'll need something done - except that when usually means now. That's is why I'm a generalist. Whenever you need something, I probably know how to help. Need a script to parse through some data, right now? Done. Is it t-shirt time? You bet it is. Time for a new logo? I can do that. Looking for economic analysis? That's what my degree's in. Think it's time your organization had a newsletter? It'll look awesome. Your day needs brightening? Adorable. Got an event coming up? It's on! Decide to make your online life more secure? I've got you covered. And I can do all that while working in payroll, Accounts Payable, or whatever else needs doing.
